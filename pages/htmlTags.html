<img>Tag

It is used to insert images in the webpage.

It is a standalone tag i.e, it does not require a closing tag.

<img src ="image.jpg" alt="image description" width="100%" height="100%" >

<a>Tag

It is used to insert links in the webpage.

It is a standalone tag i.e, it does not require a closing tag.

Links can be of texts as well as images.

<a href ="https://google.com" target="_blank" > Google </a >
